Session summary

Start a session and run for ~30–60 s to get averages and cues.

How to set up

  • Camera at hip height, fully side-on, 2–3 m away so your whole body fits.
  • Good even lighting; plain background helps tracking.
  • Wear fitted clothes — baggy clothing confuses joint detection.
  • * Bounce is an estimate scaled from your torso length, not a precise cm.
